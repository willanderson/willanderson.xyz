About

Hi, I'm Will—a designer with over 10 years of experience in product, visual, and systems design. My work spans multiple industries, from DTC e-commerce to creative tooling, and I've been fortunate to collaborate with companies such as Squarespace, Artsy, and Everlane.

I've always viewed myself as a hybrid designer, with interests spanning UX/UI, front-end development, systems-thinking, brand development, and copywriting. I'm grateful to have worked with companies that have allowed me to embrace a multidisciplinary role.

Currently, I'm at Meta, where I'm focused on building and expanding our creator and monetization tools. Before that, I held a Design Lead position at Free Association, concentrating on new product experiences for Coinbase.
