About

Hi, I'm Will—a Product Designer based in New York. For the past decade, I've created digital products, design systems, and web experiences for a range of startups, brands, and technology companies.

I'm currently at Meta, where I work on our monetization tools for creators and businesses. Before that, I held a Design Lead position at Free Association, a Brooklyn-based design firm specializing in digital products and brand systems.

I began my career as a Graphic Designer working for Madewell, where I supported the brand's print collateral, digital lookbooks, and marketing emails. In 2015, I moved out to San Francisco to join Everlane. Over the next 4 years, I helped build out the company's marketing pages, its core e-commerce experience, as well as internal tools used by our retail and merchandising teams.

In 2018, I moved back to New York and joined Squarespace. While there, I helped grow Squarespace Commerce into a leading e-commerce platform; I built out new onboarding experiences, created new logistics and payment tools, and redesigned product creation and store editing flows.

I've always considered myself a bit of a hybrid designer, with interests that span UI/UX, front-end development, product strategy, and branding. While I'm not currently taking on freelance work, I welcome the opportunity to connect—just shoot me an email.
