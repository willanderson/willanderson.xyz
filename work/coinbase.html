Coinbase

My Role

I worked contractually with Coinbase through Free Association, a design agency in Brooklyn, NY. During my time on the Coinbase account, I contributed to the redesign of Coinbase’s Design System, and collaborated with senior leadership on product vision work and feature development.

About Coinbase

Coinbase is an American publicly traded company that operates a cryptocurrency exchange platform. It is the largest cryptocurrency exchange in the United States by trading volume. The company was founded in 2012 by Brian Armstrong and Fred Ehrsam.

Unified Onboarding

I applied the Coinbase’s updated DS to their multi-product onboarding flow. I built out a set of reusable patterns that could be applied across both retail and business use cases as well as mobile and web platforms.

ENS Claim Flow

As part of my work on the Identity team, I led designs for an ENS claim flow in Coinbase’s retail and wallet apps.

Decentralized Identity

I worked with a small group of design and product leaders at Coinbase to explore a shared north star vision for Coinbase’s Web3 experience. This work included HiFi comps & prototypes for future use cases and user flows.
