Coinbase

ENS Claim Flow

While working with Coinbase’s Identity team, I designed a new integrated ENS claim flow for Coinbase Wallet. This work spanned the wallet Chrome extension, iOS wallet app, and Coinbase’s retail app. The implementation marked a significant milestone for users, enabling them to personalize their wallets and enhance P2P payments with a complimentary, easily understandable ENS domain.

Introducing users to the ENS claim flow, we focus on simplifying the concept and highlighting its benefits, paving the way for a more personalized wallet experience with an informative walkthrough.

The flow for choosing and securing an ENS name within Coinbase's iOS app.

An animation displayed during the onboarding flow. My goal was to highlight practical uses of an ENS domain, making it easier for users to see the direct benefits and applications in their digital lives.

After securing an ENS name, the next step we guide users through is setting up their profile, enhancing their wallet with a layer of personal identity that enriches peer-to-peer interactions.
