Sell It

As part of a Hack week project, I worked with a small engineering team to design and build a concept app aimed at first-time sellers. Inspired by merchants who primarily sold products through social media and private messaging, we wanted to design a tool that supported expressive online selling without the need for a store or website.

The starting point was Squarespace's existing product creation tool.

Onboarding flow

The app supported a bare-bones product creation tool that allowed users to generate products in seconds.

Products created in the app weren't uploaded to a store or website. Instead, they were hosted using a single URL.

Products could be easily distributed and accessed through social media platforms. .
